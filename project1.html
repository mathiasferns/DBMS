Track and Manage Crime Records Online

What is Crime Management System?

Crime Management System is a web-based application that helps police record and manage crime types they receive at their stations on daily basis. The project has CRUD (Create, Read, Update, and Delete) Operation functionalities.

The system allows data storage and criminal record interchange between the police. It also provides timely and accurate information on offenders, complaints, FIRs, charge sheets, prisoner records, etc. The system is designed to improve the efficiency and effectiveness of the police security measures in keeping criminal records, and to provide the crime investigator(s) with relevant information about a particular crime in a specified location .

What are the features of Crime Management System?

  • Online Complaint Application

    The system allows the public to register their complaints online and get a unique complaint number. The system also notifies the complainant about the status of their complaint and the action taken by the police.

  • Crime Record Interchange

    The system enables the police to share and access crime records across different stations and states. The system also facilitates the electronic provision of criminal record to crime investigator(s). The system ensures the security and confidentiality of the data by using encryption and authentication techniques.

  • Crime Analysis and Reporting

    The system provides various tools and reports for analyzing and visualizing the crime data. The system helps the police to identify the crime patterns, trends, hotspots, and suspects. The system also generates various reports such as crime statistics, crime types, crime locations, etc.
